About Hadamflow

Your destination for captivating movement and mindful practices. I'm Hannes, a certified poi, yoga, and gymnastics instructor dedicated to inspiring individuals and communities through dynamic workshops and performances.

From festivals and events to corporate wellness programs, I offer professional services tailored to your unique needs. My expertise, combined with a commitment to sustainability (all equipment is transported via cargo e-bike!), ensures a high-quality, engaging, and eco-conscious experience.

Let's connect and explore how we can elevate your next event or enhance your well-being.
